The best movies according to William

My top 3 movies

Wall E

This movie is very special to me. It's a movie that I know a lot of people don't prefer compared to things like Monster's Inc, or Incredibles but this movie tells such a beautiful story about humanity, nature, and love.

The Batman 2022

The batman 2022 is such a special movie. It has such a unique mood to it when compared to other superhero movies. The MCU movies at the time were all very goofy and approachable for modern audiences but the Batman was different. It told a fascinating mystery story and had such interesting characters. The characters all had reasons for existing in the world that they did. The world felt lived in and plausible in almost every way.

Dodgeball

This movie isn't anything like the other two. I wouldn't even call it good but it's a movie that means a lot to me because it's one me and my dad would always watch. I can probably quote the whole movie word for word and to this day say lines and references from the movie such as "If you can dodge a wrench, you can dodge a ball." It's also very funny and I highly reccomend you at least check it out.
